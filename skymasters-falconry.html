SkyMasters Falconry

Why Choose SkyMasters Falconry

  • Humane, chemical-free, and non-lethal solution
  • Highly visible and immediately effective deterrent
  • Environmentally responsible bird management
  • Improves safety, hygiene, and public perception
  • Fully compliant with wildlife welfare regulations

SkyMasters Falconry offers a safe and humane way to control nuisance birds in large, open, and sensitive environments where traditional deterrents may not work effectively.

Our experienced and licensed falconers use specially trained birds of prey to create a natural predator presence on site. This encourages pest birds to move away naturally, without harm, distress, or the use of chemicals.

By flying raptors strategically across the area, we disrupt nesting, roosting, and feeding patterns. This delivers immediate results while preventing birds from returning over time, making the approach highly effective, environmentally responsible, and suitable for locations where safety, hygiene, and public perception are critical.

When Falconry Is the Right Solution

  • Persistent gull, pigeon, or starling activity
  • Open sites such as schools, airports, stadia, and landfills
  • Seasonal bird surges and aggressive behaviour
  • Locations where spikes, netting, or noise deterrents fail
  • Event-based or short-term bird control requirements
