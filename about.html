Tutum Bird & Pest Management (TBPM)

Tutum Bird & Pest Management (TBPM) provides expert bird and pest control services for commercial and industrial clients. We create safe, clean, and pest-free environments using humane, eco-friendly solutions. Our certified team combines years of industry experience with modern technology to deliver tailored, long-term pest management. We also offer a rapid, same-day response, including free, no-obligation site surveys carried out by a certified surveyor for complete reassurance.

Our Mission

To deliver safe, effective, and sustainable pest and bird control solutions while maintaining the highest standards of customer service, safety, and environmental responsibility.

Environmental Commitment

TBPM is committed to reducing environmental impact through green products, non-lethal bird control methods, and waste-reduction initiatives.

Our Managing Director

Michael Jacobs, Managing Director of TBPM, brings over 15 years of experience in commercial insurance and environmental consultancy. Known for his ethical and sustainable approach, he has built a strong client base across London and the Home Counties and is committed to creating future employment opportunities.
