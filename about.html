Our Approach

Every client engagement begins with a free, no-obligation site survey carried out by a certified surveyor. We assess risks, compliance obligations, and long-term prevention opportunities before designing a bespoke management plan.

Rather than one-size-fits-all treatments, we deliver scalable solutions built on best practice, transparency, and ongoing accountability.

Meet Our Managing Director

Michael Jacobs

Managing Director, Tutum

Michael brings a rare combination of strategic insight, technical understanding, and commercial acumen to the bird and pest control sector.

Beginning his career in the commercial insurance market, Michael spent over 15 years as a Senior Global Property Underwriter in the City of London, advising Risk Managers, Finance Directors, and CEOs on complex, high-value portfolios.

Prior to founding Tutum, he worked as a Contract Manager for a national bird and pest control company across London, managing client relationships, site surveys, quotations, and project delivery. He holds the RSPH Level 2 qualification in Pest Management.

His transition into environmental markets consulting strengthened his commitment to sustainability, ultimately leading to the creation of a purpose-driven bird and pest management business.

  • Customer-centric, consultative leadership
  • Strong ethical standards and transparency
  • Sustainable, innovative pest management
  • Long-term, trusted client relationships
